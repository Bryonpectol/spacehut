Space Hut

Observe outer space from the comfort of your own

hut.


Our goal is to create an environment where users around the world can learn about, view, and discover our universe and outer space. From anywhere in the world. Space Hut covers everything from rockets launched into space, to the edge of the universe and even beyond. For now, there is only the Space Hut website. But in the near future, we will create a Space Hut phone app that will be completely offline for easy access wherever the user may be. We will eventually create a Space Hut magazine if we can get enough funding to do so. These are our goals to help our viewers learn about the Universe, whenever and wherever they are.
